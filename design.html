Graphic Design

My graphic design work ranges from promotional flyers, social media graphics, to package design. In most of my design projects, I incorporate my personal photography work and aim to convey feelings through the use of fonts, shapes and colors.



I work in Adobe Creative Suite: Photoshop, Illustrator, and AfterEffects, Clip Studio Paint, and Canva.



Animated Flyers



I designed various flyers and graphics for my organization, Alpha Kappa Psi. These graphics were posted on social media, such as Facebook, Instagram, and our public website. I worked with our recruitment chairs to create flyers to increase interest for our recruitment events. These flyers were posted on Instagram, and garnered an average of around 200 views each. They increased daily profile clicks by an average of 20.





Professional Series



Every semester, our organization hosted a professional week, a week of events geared towards professional development of its attendees. I created a short series of informative graphics on Instagram, with the intent of sharing professional advice, such as how to build a resume, and how to prepare for a job interview. The series followed the 5 W's: who, what, when, where, why and how. The goal was to help students prepare for their future jobs and careers.

I planned, designed, and wrote text for the following graphics. The photographs used in these graphics were taken and edited by me.

Brand Packaging



As part of a marketing project, I worked on packaging design for a bar soap. I drew inspiration from pastel and soft-themed candle packaging. Surprisingly, it worked well for the purpose. The following packages are designed for different scented soaps: apricot, watermelon, lime, and lavender.
