The Silver Swan

BLENDER


A term project requiring the use of Blender to tell an informative but concise, 30-second long story with audio and visuals.

By using Blender and Audacity, I created a short video about The Silver Swan automaton of the Bowes Museum.

Character Sheet

ADOBE PHOTOSHOP


I created a character sheet for the silver swan to measure its dimensions and proportions to aid me for the 3D modeling.

3D Model



Using geometric objects, I sculpted a model of the character, shot from four different angles.

Audio Recording

AUDACITY


Writing and recording a script to produce a layered audio track for supplementing the animation.

Scene Building

BLENDER


I modeled other objects and added them to the scene, as well as a 360 degree virtual background.

Animation

BLENDER


The animation, about 15 seconds long, features my character interacting with its environment.

Final Video

BLENDER


Using Blender as a video editor, I pieced together everything to create the final product. Watch it here.
